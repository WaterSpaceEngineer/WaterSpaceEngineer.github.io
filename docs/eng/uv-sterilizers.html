Ultraviolet water sterilizer
Water Space

Features

  • 1. Resistance to surges and voltage drops

    Control units designed and manufactured with electromagnetic ballasts, which, unlike electronic ones, are fully resistant to voltage surges and drops.

  • 2. Increased efficiency of water irradiation

    Our sterilizers utilize «whirl & mixтм»technology generating water vortex turbulization along the reactor. The result is - maximization of water irradiation efficiency and bactericidal water treatment performance.

  • 3. Ease of maintenance

    Construction design and «quick off»connection allow quick and easy replacement of germicidal lamp either from top or bottom sections of the reactor without additional tools, detachments nor pressure relief of the water supply.

  • 4. UV lamp power intensity control

    UV Sterilizers, model PRO, equipped with continuous UV lamp power intensity monitoring unit, eliminating any need to purchase additional expensive sensors.

  • 5. Minimal water heating effect in the reactor

    The systems designed to ensure minimization of water heating effect in chamber, even during long operating hours in standby mode.
