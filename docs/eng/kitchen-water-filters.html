Water filters for under the sink

Water Space water filters provide water purification from:

  • mechanical impurities (sand, rust, silt, scale, etc.);

  • hardness salts, organic and organochlorine compounds, heavy and non-ferrous metals, phenols and petroleum products

  • improve the organoleptic properties of water (color, taste, smell).

The filters are designed for apartments with 3-4 family members and combine efficiency and practicality of use.

The filters are made of virgin food grade material (polypropylene), which complies with the EU Directive "Materials and objects intended to come into contact with food products".

The filter is connected to the cold-water supply pipeline and can be equipped (at the customer's choice) with a separate tap, which is mounted on the sink or kitchen worktop.
