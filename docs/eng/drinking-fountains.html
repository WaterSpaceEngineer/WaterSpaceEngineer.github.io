Drinking Fountains

Used in:

  • Preschool, educational and medical institutions

  • Shopping malls, entertainment, sports complexes, fitness centers and other places of mass traffic

  • Offices, municipals, industrial premises and other public facilities

  • Park areas, recreation areas, etc.

Use of drinking fountains together with our water treatment systems is a ready solution for providing any public places with high-quality drinking water. The complex and selective water purification process implemented in our systems preserves the minerals necessary for the human body, especially for the growing body of a child, while removing unnecessary and harmful substances.

The design of our drinking fountains makes maintenance simple and easy.
