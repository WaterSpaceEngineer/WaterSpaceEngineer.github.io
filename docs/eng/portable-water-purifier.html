Portable filtering system for complex water purification. Designed with The Defense Forces of Ukraine in mind.

Portable water filtration system WS-C-090650-UF10C

The filter designed to obtain high-quality drinking water from any natural reservoirs, including highly polluted ones.

The filter is easy to use, does not require power source (water is supplied by a built-in hand pump), compact, adapted to field conditions (packed in a special military-style case).

The filter carries out complex water purification and, due to its high performance, can provide drinking water to a military unit (up to a company) or a tourist group for a long period of time. Regeneration of the filter compound is carried out with a 10% sodium chloride solution.

The unit performs chemical and microbiological water purification, removing a large array of contaminants: organic and organochlorine compounds, nitrates, nitrites, fluorides, phosphates, sulfates, bromides, iron, manganese, calcium, magnesium, copper, lead, zinc, mercury, aluminum; bacteria, fungi, helminths, simple micro organisms and viruses.
