AISI 430 stainless steel profiles and angles (curved profile)

Profiles and corners are available in two types of surface finish - mirror polished or brushed (satin). The outer surface is covered with a protective film that is removed immediately after installation.

The standard length is 2 m. Standard wall thickness 0.8; 1; 1.5 mm.

Customer orders available. You can order any required parameters (bending angle, flange width, flange thickness). For custom orders, please request pricing.

Minimum order when shipping is 10 m.

Applications:

  • production of light metal structures with anti-corrosion properties

  • protection of structural elements from damage

  • edging of countertops, stairs, furniture elements

  • decorative finishing of various products

  • design elements for tiling, clapboard, etc.
