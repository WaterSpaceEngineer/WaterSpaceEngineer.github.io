Automatic uninterruptible power supply system (UPS)

Designed for uninterruptible power supply for electrical appliances, telecommunications equipment and backup lighting.

Two choices for modes of operation:

  • 1. Automatic – the backup power is turned on automatically in the event of a power failure in the network.

  • 2. Manual – the power is turned on manually using the switch on the unit.

With Automatic mode selected, in the event of network power failure the power supplied by the UPS delivered within 0.0005 of a second. Prompt power delivery ensures that connected equipment does not reboot. The battery is charged automatically and a completely discharged unit reaches its full charge within 2 hours.

Mounting – wall mount. The entire high-voltage part of the unit is encased in an additional protective casing to prevent electric shock during maintenance.

UPS Model 12-220-1300PS

UPS 220 V, 1300 W is designed for backup power supply of electrical appliances with a voltage of 220 V and a frequency of 50 Hz from a lead-acid battery with a voltage of 12 V.

The output signal of the device is a pure sine wave, which allows it to be used with almost any electrical appliance. Can be used with any types of lead-acid accumulators. Comes with a built-in charger.

Indications: output voltage, output current, output power, power factor, temperature, battery level, battery voltage, standby, fault.

UPS for lighting and telecommunications equipment

Provides power to routers, modems, switches, media converters, video surveillance devices, etc., as well as emergency lighting for all premises, home and office.

Equipped with AGM battery (12 V, 7 Ah), the unit will help you to maintain uninterrupted access to the Internet and simultaneous lighting of premises for up to 12 hours. Supplied with a block of self-clamping connectors for quick connection of light fixtures to DC 5.5×2.1 outputs and a set of cables required for connecting home or office equipment.

The unit comes with a pulse, microprocessor-based charger with a LED display, to provide you with information about battery charge level, terminal voltage, charging current and battery capacity.

The body material: polyester-coated steel.
