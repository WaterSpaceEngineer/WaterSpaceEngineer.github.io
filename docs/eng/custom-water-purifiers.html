Custom Water Purification Systems

Our company provides services of evaluation, configuration and installation of water purification equipment. The evaluation of the equipment requirements is based on the source waters analysis, which can be carried out by our laboratory. The complete equipment setup and configuration takes into account the location of installation and specific customer needs. Prefabricated frame, bactericidal water treatment systems and any additional devices and components are manufactured by our company.
