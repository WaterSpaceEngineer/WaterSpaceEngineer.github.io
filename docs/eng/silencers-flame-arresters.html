Silencer and Muzzle Blast Suppression System

Designed to mask the shooting position by reducing and distorting the sound of a shot and suppressing the muzzle blast. Can be used with AK, RPK, PKM of all modifications. With special ammunition (for example, 5.45 УС cartridges with black and green mark), the system can be used for silent shooting. With standard ammunition, the volume of the shot is reduced by 50-60%, while the tone of the sound changes, which makes it impossible to detect the shooting position by ear.

OEM System.

Comes with all instructions and passports.

Frequently asked question: Why are there no grooves for a spring-loaded pin lock? Answer: Such latches are incompatible with our system. This is due to a very precise centering of the system. The suppressor must be tightly screwed onto the barrel without even slightest play. Therefore, attach it tightly and action! After shooting 10-20 rounds, when the system’s body has warmed up, tighten further (be careful, it is hot). Once tightened, the system is fully set and won’t go anywhere – tested and verified!

The system has an adaptive fixation mechanism to the barrel, which does not require individual adjustment and use of any additional tools for installation.

The device is screwed on to the stop, the ring of the fixating mechanism is rotated to the stop and is clamped with a locking screw. The locking mechanism prevents the device from getting loose at any intensity of fire.
