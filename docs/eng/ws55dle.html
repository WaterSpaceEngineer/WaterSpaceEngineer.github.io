Ultraviolet water Sterilizer
Model WS55DLE

Advantages:

  • Control units designed and manufactured with electromagnetic ballasts, which, unlike electronic ones, are fully resistant to voltage surges and drops,

  • Water turbulization technology whirl & mix™ ensures maximization of water irradiation efficiency and bactericidal water treatment performance,

  • Construction design and the «quick off» connector allow swift replacement of germicidal lamp which can be executed either from the top or from the bottom of the reactor without additional tools and without detachment nor pressure relief of the water supply,

  • Hermetic connections design of the UV lamp prevents corrosion effects, which may afflict damage to the electrical connections due to condensates in quartz case,

  • Overall design ensures minimization of water heating effect in chamber, even during long operat-ing hours in standby mode.
