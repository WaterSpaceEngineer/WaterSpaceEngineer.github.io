Integrated Water Purification Systems

Designed for chemical and microbiological (UV Irradiation) water purification, removing the following: organic and organochlorine compounds, nitrates, nitrites, fluorides, phosphates, sulfates, bromides, iron, manganese, calcium, magnesium, copper, lead, zinc, mercury, aluminum; bacteria, fungi, helminths, protozoa, viruses.

Correct use and maintenance of the system provides high quality of the treated water, that meets all the requirements of Standard for drinking water.

Filter regeneration is carried out automatically during night hours.

Our Services:

  • 1.Initial consultation.

  • 2.Laboratory examination of source water (can be performed by our laboratory).

  • 3.Selection of required filters and equipment production.

  • 4.Equipment installation at the client’s facility (by the client or by our specialists).

  • 5.Warranty and post-warranty service.
