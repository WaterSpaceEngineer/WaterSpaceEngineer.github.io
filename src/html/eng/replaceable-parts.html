Ultraviolet Irradiators (bactericidal lamps)

Water Space sterilizers equipped with German T8 bactericidal lamps (UV lamps) manufactured by Osram company. These irradiators are highly reliable and efficient in treating large volumes of water (compared to T5 lamps).

The product is certified, which confirms its strength and durability.

Models WS15D/P UV, WS20D/P UV-C

  • Model WS15D/P UV-C for sterilizers WS15DLE, WS15DE, WS15D PRO

  • Model WS20D/P UV-C for sterilizers WS20DLE, WS20DE, WS20D PRO

Technical Specs:

  • UV wave lenght – 254 nm,

  • Life time – 9000 hours,

  • Power:

    • Model WS15D/P UV-C – 15 Вт,
    • Model WS20D/P UV-C – 20 Вт,

  • Lamp socket – 4-pin,

  • Dimensions - 467×31 mm,

  • Weight-net – 0.11 kg,

  • Weight-gross – 0.2 kg

  • Kit contains: Irradiator (UV lamp), Transport sleeve, Documentation.
