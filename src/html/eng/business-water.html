Water Retail Enterprise

Water Space – equipment specially designed for the business of production and sale of drinking water. We combined best global practices and more than 20 years of our experience in the production of a variety of equipment for water treatment and water use.

ОWater treatment equipment and water sales counters can be purchased in our standard version or can be custom designed as per your needs.

The design and technological solutions implemented in our equipment fundamentally solve an array of important tasks faced by businesses: production of high-quality drinking water from tap waters minimizing the costs of purchase, operation and maintenance of the equipment.

Thanks to the technological optimization of the water treatment process, we eliminated reverse osmosis filters from our systems as such filters are useless and even can be harmful (read about it here ). With exclusion of reverse osmosis filters storage tanks and pumps are no longer needed. Such a solution also drastically minimized the volumes of technical waters discharge during regeneration of filtering fills.

Furthermore, the compact dimensions of the equipment minimize the room size required for placement, while its mobility allows painless transfer from place to place. This helps entrepreneurs to significantly reduce real estate rental costs and provides maneuverability within a store.

Remote control over water bottling allows equipment placement within existing stores allowing entrepreneurs to utilize pre-existing store set up and employees eliminating the need for additional employees and associated taxation burden.

With such high productivity (for this type of business) of 500 – 1000 liters per hour, our equipment drastically minimizes drinking water production costs: 0.005 UAH per 1 liter.

Our equipment provides a number of advantages: ease of use, attractive design, durability, and minimal maintenance costs.

WaterSpace design and technologies target to minimize your costs of ownership and operation as well as enable you to provide your customers with high-quality of drinking water. Our equipment is widely represented in the market of drinking water sales, for the quality of which customers vote with their returning busineeng

  • 1. Equipment purchase and installation.

  • 2. We sign a franchise agreement, providing you with:

  • 5. We provide ongoing technical and methodological support for your business.
