Water dispensers (bottling) for drinking water retail enterprises

Dispensers/Counters designed for commercial water bottling with full sales and revenue monitoring and reporting system. The design provides an adjustable filling nozzle for various bottle dimensions with precise metering of dispensed volumes. In addition, the dispensers/counters provide dual-channel system that allows separated catering of two water taste types.

Water dispensing can be executed in manual or automatic mode.

The dispensers/counters can be connected to an existing water purification system or combining with our Water Space Purification Systems.

Functions

  • programmable price per liter (password protected)

  • automatic dosing of dispensed volume

  • accounting and storage of data on the dispensed volumes for a reporting period

  • accounting and storage of data on sales for a reporting period

  • data protection - data reset only by the person in charge using a physical key

  • filling accuracy calibration

  • indication of the volume of dispensed water

  • indication of the cost of dispensed water (the amount to be received from the buyer)

  • price indication per liter

  • data entry with a compact, waterproof matrix keyboard

  • gradation of automatic dosing from 0.1 liter
